In His Own Words
"Somebody asked me why I love grilling so much… and honestly, I had to think about it."
— The Grill Master

It's more than just cooking food. It's about creating moments — bringing people together, hosting gatherings, and serving something made with love straight from the grill.

From intimate gatherings to events serving 100+ guests, we show up ready to make your occasion one to remember. Every event is cooked live, on-site — no warming trays, no shortcuts, no reheated food. Just fresh BBQ, made right in front of the people we're feeding.

We also offer meal prep services for those who want delicious, freshly grilled meals without the hassle. Whether it's one event or weekly prep, McCollum Grilling Co. brings the same care and quality every single time.
