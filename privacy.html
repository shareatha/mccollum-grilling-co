Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

McCollum Grilling Co. ("McCollum Grilling Co.," "MGC," "we," "us," or "our") operates this website and the booking form on it. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you request a quote or contact us, how we use and store it, and the choices you have.

By submitting the booking form on this website, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

Information We Collect

When you fill out our booking form to request a quote, we collect the information you provide, which may include:

  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Event type, state, and date
  • Expected headcount
  • Menu interests and any custom menu requests
  • Preferred contact method (email, phone, or Instagram DM)
  • Any additional details you share about your event

We do not collect payment information through this website, and we do not use cookies, analytics, or advertising trackers on this site.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you submit solely to:

  • Respond to your quote request and follow up about your event
  • Plan menu, staffing, and logistics for your booking
  • Communicate with you before, during, and after your event

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

How Your Information Is Stored

Booking form submissions are recorded in a private Google Sheet that is accessible only to authorized McCollum Grilling Co. admin team members. This sheet is not publicly accessible and is used internally to manage and respond to booking requests.

To make sure a submission isn't missed, a copy of each booking request is also sent as an email notification to our team through a third-party form-delivery service. That service transmits the notification but does not use your information for any purpose of its own.

Third-Party Services

We rely on the following third-party services to operate our booking form and store submissions. Each is bound by its own privacy practices:

  • Google (Google Sheets / Google Workspace) — used to store and manage booking submissions. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Web3Forms — used to deliver an email notification of each booking request to our team. See Web3Forms' Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

We retain booking information for as long as reasonably necessary to manage your event and maintain business records, or until you request that we delete it. If you'd like your information removed sooner, contact us using the details below.

Your Choices

You may contact us at any time to:

  • Request a copy of the information we have on file for you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request that we delete your information from our records

We'll respond to reasonable requests as quickly as we can.

Children's Privacy

This website and booking form are intended for adults planning events and are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

We restrict access to booking submissions to authorized MGC admin team members and rely on the built-in security of the third-party platforms noted above. No method of storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect the information you share with us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, reach out to us at events@mccollumgrillingco.com or via Instagram @mccollumgrillingco.
